How It Works

  1. Drop your item(s) into ShrinkSack.
  2. Press the badge. The bag fills with micro-compression field.
  3. Watch the item shrink to a tiny parcel, then zip it away.
  4. Unzip and release when needed. Item returns to full size instantly.
